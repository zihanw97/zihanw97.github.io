Course

ME 4350 - Mechanical Computer-Aided Engineering

4 credits | Generally offered fall

Introduction to the use of state-of-the-art finite element technology in mechanical engineering analysis. Fundamentals of computer graphics, solid modeling, finite element modeling, and interactive design. Analysis and evaluation of linear static and dynamic mechanical systems. Includes a project.

Additional requirement: Major standing

Prerequisites: ME 3300 and ME 3250
