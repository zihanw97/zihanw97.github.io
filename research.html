Research

Research directions

AIM Lab advances AI-enabled, experimentally grounded modeling and design methods for manufacturing, materials discovery, metamaterials, and robust mechanical systems.

01

Autonomous design and discovery

Closed-loop workflows that integrate high-throughput simulation, experiment, and adaptive learning for smart manufacturing and materials discovery.

02

Generative co-design

Deep generative and graph-based methods for designing manufacturable mechanical and material systems, including porous and cellular metamaterials.

03

Uncertainty-aware design

Probabilistic modeling, Bayesian optimization, and robust design approaches that account for variability, heterogeneity, and reliability in engineered systems.
