Join & Contact

Opportunities and contact

Students and collaborators interested in AI-enabled manufacturing and materials discovery are welcome to connect with the AIM Lab.

Graduate students

The AIM Lab is interested in students excited by machine learning, design optimization, mechanics, manufacturing, materials science, and scientific computing.

Undergraduate researchers

Motivated undergraduate students can contribute to simulation, data generation, visualization, and experimental or computational research projects.

Collaborators

We welcome collaborations on autonomous experimentation, materials discovery, robust design, digital twins, additive manufacturing, and metamaterial systems.

Reach Out

Start a conversation

Prospective students can send a short introduction, research interests, CV, transcript, and relevant project or publication links. Collaborators are welcome to share a brief note about how their interests connect with AIM Lab's research directions.
